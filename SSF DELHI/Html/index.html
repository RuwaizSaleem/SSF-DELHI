About

A creative nation is created when the students' creative abilities can be applied purposefully. The mission that SSF has been carrying out in Kerala for the past half a century is to prepare a ripe ground for students to come together socially, culturally and ideologically. The cadre system of state, district, division, sector, unit and block is working from the realization that the student period is a boat that cannot be rowed until the right and wrong are recognized.

The SSF has been able to integrate with the mainstream society and express itself with more diversity in various fields such as religious, social and cultural. SSF strives to re-invigorate diverse spaces like education, struggles, discussions, debates, art fairs with fresh water and manure. SSF is taking root in the field of religious and social services in states including Kerala.
